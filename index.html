Know Your Block

Kurkheda Block is situated in Gadchiroli District, Maharashtra, India. Known for its rich tribal culture, the region is predominantly rural with a focus on agriculture and forestry. The area is characterized by lush landscapes, diverse flora, and a close-knit community that values traditional practices and sustainable living.
